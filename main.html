Part II — Master Set: Top 30 Hardest Questions

Algebra & Number Theory

Q1. Find all pairs of positive integers \((x,y)\) such that \(x^2+5x+6=2^y\).
Q2. If \(a+b+c=0\) and \(a^2+b^2+c^2=1\), find \(a^4+b^4+c^4\).
Q3. Find the remainder when \(x^{2023}-2x^{2022}+3x-5\) is divided by \((x-1)(x-2)\).
Q4. Solve \(\sqrt{x+3-4\sqrt{x-1}}+\sqrt{x+8-6\sqrt{x-1}}=1\).
Q5. If \(f(x)+2f(1/x)=3x\), find \(f(x)\).

Q9. Find \(A^n\) for \(A=\begin{pmatrix}1&1\\0&1\end{pmatrix}\).
